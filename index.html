Virtue.


Virtue opposes vice, it has great importance to ethical life. Vice is any practice, conduct or habit that is considered a fault, a defect, a disease, or a bad habit.

Aims


To be where we want, first we been working and building one path. Imagine if we are looking to horizon and we are not realizing that we are not having a path to set foot, it would be just a dream. With this analogy I pretend explain the daily day when we are working on a project. We want first finish the project before started. We are not realizing that if we not know the process and the tools we not advance to our aims. So we need to parse what knowledge we needed. Despair only bring us waste of time and frustration. If we have a doubt of how to do something start making questions of what we need to do to reach our aims. Try to resolve a little issue that is important to advance a little bit more and then step by step you can reach your goal. Some issues take more time than others so take your time to figure it out.
